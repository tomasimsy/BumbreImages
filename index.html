About Me

Hello, I’m Tom and I’m a wedding photographer based in Charlotte, North Carolina. I have been photographing weddings for over 5 years, and I love every aspect of it. From the engagement session to the big day, I strive to capture the emotions, details, and moments that make each wedding unique and special.

I have a natural and candid style of photography, with a touch of creativity and elegance. I use a combination of natural light and flash to create beautiful and flattering images. I also edit my photos with care and attention, to enhance the colors and mood of each scene.

I work closely with my clients to understand their vision and expectations for their wedding photography. I offer a variety of packages and services to suit different needs and budgets. I also provide a personalized online gallery, a high-resolution USB drive, and a custom-designed album for each wedding.

I’m honored and grateful to be a part of one of the most important days in people’s lives. I enjoy meeting new couples and building lasting relationships with them. I’m always happy to hear from them after the wedding, and see how their love story continues.

I’m Tom, and I’m a wedding photographer. If you’re looking for someone to capture your special day, please feel free to contact me. I would love to hear from you!
